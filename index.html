Foodafy/Project 2

For project 2, our group created an app calld Foodafy. Foodafy is an online application that allows the user to house recipes. After logging in and adding the meal you are looking to prepare, a slide appears with the recipe, calories, and diet labels i.e. low carb etc. The app will keep the recipes on your page unless you decide to remove them.

There is a login page where the email and password is required. You may enter any email address and password to access the tool. As a future enhancement, we plan to hook up the MySQL table from the recipes to the MySQL table attached to the login page using a foreign key and create a page that logs the users recipe logs.

Scraper News

In this app you will be able to view and leave comments on the latest news by locating the "Scrape" button in the upper right corner of the navigation bar. Click into the article you want to read and you will see a field to enter your name and a field to leave a comment. After entering this information, click the "SUBMIT" button to log the comment information. You can clear all the articles and start over by hitting the "Clear Articles" button next to the "Scrape" button in the navigation bar.
